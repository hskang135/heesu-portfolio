Projects

Spaced Repetition

The Spaced Repetition is an application that users can learn 10 basic Spanish words and can take a practice word anytime when they are ready for it. Users able to check their answer is correct or incorrect directly on the learning page. Also, Spaced Repetition will display how many times they got correct or incorrect answers for each word and total score on the dashboard page. I hope many users use our Spaced Repetition to improve their Spanish word knowledge skills.
